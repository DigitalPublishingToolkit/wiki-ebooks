Wiki Ebooks

The free encyclopedia Wikipedia and its sister projects in the Wikimedia family (such as the free media base Wikimedia Commons and the free knowledge base Wikidata) are used by millions of people daily. Their content is mainly consulted online and is not (yet) frequently re-used in e-books. However, there is quite a bit of potential for interesting and useful e-books based on Wikimedia content. How can we unlock this potential?

From September 2016 till January 2017, the PublishingLab in Amsterdam researches the potential of curated e-books that are based on (distributed) Wikimedia content from various projects, such as Wikipedia, Wikidata, Wikimedia Commons, Wikibooks, Wikisource, and more.
